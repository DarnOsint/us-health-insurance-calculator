## About

The **US Health Insurance Calculator** is a free, browser-based tool that helps you estimate your health insurance costs under the **Affordable Care Act (ACA)** marketplace. Whether you're shopping during **open enrollment** or exploring your options, this calculator provides instant estimates for:

- **Premium Tax Credit** (subsidy) eligibility based on your household income and Federal Poverty Level (FPL)
- **Monthly premium** estimates after subsidies for Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum plan tiers
- **Cost-Sharing Reduction (CSR)** eligibility for Silver plans
- **HSA (Health Savings Account)** eligibility with High-Deductible Health Plans (HDHP)
- Expected contribution as a percentage of income
- **Deductible**, **copay**, and **coinsurance** estimates by plan tier

This tool uses 2025 Federal Poverty Level guidelines and the enhanced ACA subsidy structure extended through the Inflation Reduction Act.

## Features

- **Household Income & Size Input** -- Enter your expected annual income and number of household members
- **Age-Based Premium Estimates** -- Add ages for each household member for accurate benchmark premium calculations
- **State Selection** -- Choose your state (premiums vary by location)
- **Tobacco Use Adjustment** -- Tobacco surcharge factor included in estimates
- **FPL Percentage Calculation** -- Instantly see where your household falls on the Federal Poverty Level scale
- **Premium Tax Credit / Subsidy Estimate** -- Based on 2025 ACA contribution percentages (0% to 8.5% of income)
- **Plan Tier Comparison** -- Side-by-side comparison of Bronze (60% AV), Silver (70% AV), Gold (80% AV), and Platinum (90% AV) plans
- **Cost-Sharing Reduction (CSR) Eligibility** -- For Silver plan enrollees under 250% FPL
- **HSA Eligibility Check** -- Identifies if you may qualify for a Health Savings Account with a High-Deductible Health Plan
- **Dark Theme UI** -- Easy on the eyes, fully responsive design
- **100% Client-Side** -- No data sent to any server; runs entirely in your browser

## How to Use

1. **Open `index.html`** in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
2. **Enter your household income** (annual gross income before taxes)
3. **Set household size** and enter the age of each member
4. **Select your state** from the dropdown
5. **Indicate tobacco use** if applicable
6. **Click "Calculate"** to see your results
7. Review your **FPL percentage**, **estimated subsidy**, **monthly premiums** by plan tier, and **CSR eligibility**

No installation, no sign-up, no data collection. Just open and use.

## Technical Details

- **Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript** -- no frameworks, no dependencies
- **2025 Federal Poverty Level**: $15,060 for a single person + $5,380 for each additional household member
- **ACA Enhanced Subsidies**: Extended contribution percentages from 0% to 8.5% of income for households 100-400%+ FPL
- **Benchmark Silver Premium**: Age-adjusted estimates from $350 to $800/month
